In this project, I created a scenario where:

  • An Ice-cream shop has a selected stock of options in which a customer can choose from (stocks)
  • The customer can then make an order (customerOrder).
  • Then customer can place an order (order).
  • then when the order is received and processed, production begins (production)
  • Then when production finishes, the customer receives their Ice-cream Yayy!

I used a method called callback hell. Ouch!

Make sure to check out the inspect element, console.
